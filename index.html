General Tips

  • Getting 2 shields and the ability to fight against 2 shields is crucial in the early game
    • Getting 2 shields early can result in some enemies being unable to damage you, leading to them being free scrap. Conversely, being unable to damage a ship with two shields is a recipe for an early run end.
  • It's better to spend resources than Hull Points
    • Hull points are expensive to repair, and you can often get another missile or drone part sooner than a hull point.
  • Delay Reactor Upgrades
    • You don't need to keep the Medbay powered constantly. Same goes for O2, if you can keep track of your O2 levels. Also, If you have 2 shields, 2 engines, and 4 power for the whole group, then until sector 3, it is probably worth not upgrading the reactor.
  • Prefer Crew Kills
    • Crew killing an enemy ship gives you more scrap, more resources, and better chances for free crew and equipment.
